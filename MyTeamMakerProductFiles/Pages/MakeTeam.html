Make A Team!

In the section below, you can make your teams!
First, choose your preferred mode of input. If you have used this website before, and want to import data, you can use the "Import Data" button. If you want to start afresh, you can use the "Enter Data" Button. This will open a form, which you need to fill. You can choose any number of attributes that will form the basis of team formation. You will then have to name these attributes (for your convinience) and subsequently, enter the data (name and score for each attribute) of all the people you wish to include in the team process. There is no limit to the maximum score for each attribute. However, we recommend setting it as 10 or 100. Nevertheless, it can be anything best suited to you (you do not need to enter this maximum).

Sometimes, you may want to have people with different skills in the same group. In that case, simply click on the button "Make Teams Reversed" when it appears and you have filled all the spaces! If you are uploading the data and want to choose more diverse teams, then click on the Upload Data Reversed button.

The site does not accept the characters: ", : % \".
